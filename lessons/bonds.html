Stocks: Own a Piece of the Action

A stock is a share of ownership in a company. When you buy a stock, its value can grow if the company does well!

  • Example: Buying Tesla stock means you own a tiny part of Tesla.
  • Key Idea: Prices change based on company success and market trends.
